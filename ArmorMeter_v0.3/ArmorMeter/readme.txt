ArmorMeter


ArmorMeter shows the effects of armor reducing spells on the targeted mob. Each spell on the target will generated estimated damage gained. Use command /am to toggle the display of the GUI on and off.

INSTALLATION:

Extract the files contained in this archive to your addon folder (worldofwarcraft\interface\addon).
